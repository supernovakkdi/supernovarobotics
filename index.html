About Supernova Robotics

Founded in 2020, Supernova Robotics is dedicated to inspiring and educating the next generation of innovators through hands-on robotics training. Our mission is to make cutting-edge technology accessible to young minds and help them develop the skills needed for the future.

Our team of experienced educators and robotics professionals is passionate about creating an engaging learning environment where students can explore, create, and innovate. We believe in learning by doing, problem-solving through teamwork, and celebrating creativity.
